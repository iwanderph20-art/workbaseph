For Employers
WorkBase PH is a curated matchmaking platform — not a job board. Instead of posting a job and waiting for applications to flood in, we connect you directly with pre-vetted Filipino specialists who are the right fit for your role, culture, and standards. We do the filtering so you only deal with qualified candidates.

You have two paths:

Option 0 — Pay Per Post ($15 per job post): Post a single job and receive applications directly. Perfect for one-off or project-based hiring. No subscription needed.

Option 1 — The Marketplace ($59/month, cancel anytime): Self-service access to our full database of pre-verified Philippine Specialists. Browse, interview, and hire directly — no placement fees or middlemen.

Option 2 — Elite Headhunting (flat service fee + success fee based on salary bracket): A fully managed, done-for-you search. We handle sourcing, testing, portfolio audits, skills assessments, and preliminary interviews. You receive a curated shortlist of the top candidates only. Initiated with a refundable $500 deposit.

The $500 deposit initiates the Elite Headhunting search. It is fully refundable if we are unable to produce a qualified shortlist within the agreed timeframe. If the search results in a successful hire, the $500 is 100% deducted from the final placement fee (a flat service fee plus a success fee based on the hired specialist's salary bracket). The deposit is non-refundable if you withdraw the role after sourcing and vetting has already begun.
The Elite Headhunting fee is made up of two parts: a flat service fee plus a success fee based on the hired specialist's salary bracket. It is only charged once the candidate signs an offer — you pay nothing during the search. The $500 deposit you paid upfront is fully deducted from the final fee at the time of placement.
Elite Headhunting clients are covered by a 60-Day Replacement Guarantee. If the placed specialist does not meet your standards or leaves within 60 calendar days of the engagement start date, we conduct a full replacement search at no additional cost. WorkBase PH handles the off-boarding and brings in the replacement — you don't have to deal with any tough conversations.

For Specialists
Yes — 100% free. WorkBase PH earns exclusively from employer subscription and placement fees. We never charge specialists a registration fee, commission, or platform fee. Every peso you earn through a WorkBase PH match belongs entirely to you.
After you complete your profile — including your personality video, assessment, and verified readiness documents — employers either browse you on the Marketplace or request your profile through our Elite Headhunting service. You don't apply for jobs; employers come to you. The stronger and more complete your profile, the more likely you are to be shortlisted.
The Personality Assessment is a 20-question work-style profile that reveals your communication strengths, work preferences, and best-fit employer type. Your results permanently appear on your profile and are visible to every employer who views you. It cannot be re-taken or removed once submitted — so take your time and answer honestly. This is what makes our matching smarter than a standard job board.
The WorkBase Verified badge is awarded to specialists who complete all proof-of-readiness requirements: a Get-to-Know-Me video, an internet speed test result, a workspace photo, and a system specifications screenshot. These items signal to employers that you are a serious, professional remote worker — not just a resume on a screen.
Record a 5–10 minute personality reel using Loom, YouTube (unlisted), or Vocaroo. Talk about who you are, how you work, what you're great at, and what kind of employer or project you're looking for. Be real — authenticity wins. If you're camera-shy, a voice recording over your work samples works too. Make sure your audio is clear and your environment is clean and professional.
When an employer wants to interview you, they send two available time slots directly to your Inbox inside the WorkBase PH dashboard. You'll see a notification bell icon light up. Open your Inbox, review both time options, and click Confirm on the one that works best for you. Once confirmed, a Jitsi video meeting link is automatically generated and shared with both you and the employer. No email chains. No scheduling tools. Everything happens inside the platform.
