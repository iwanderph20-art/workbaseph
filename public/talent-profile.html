Get-to-Know-Me Video Highly Recommended

Let employers see the real you before the first interview. Record a 5–10 minute reel — talk about who you are, how you work, and what you're great at. Authenticity wins every time.

Go to vocaroo.com, click Record, introduce yourself in 2–5 minutes, then click Stop and copy the shareable link.

Privacy reminder: Before recording, check your screen, background, and documents for sensitive information. Blur or redact: client names, contract amounts, passwords, personal IDs, and internal URLs. Your recording will be visible to prospective employers — you can update or remove it anytime.
