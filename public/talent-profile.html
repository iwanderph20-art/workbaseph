Personality Video Highly Recommended

Let employers see the real you before the first interview. Record a 5–10 minute reel — talk about who you are, how you work, and what you're great at. Employers respond best to genuine, natural presentations.

Privacy reminder: Before recording, please review your screen, documents, and background for any sensitive information. Redact or blur: full names of clients or colleagues, company names you're not authorized to disclose, contract amounts, internal URLs, passwords, personal ID numbers, and any other private or confidential data. WorkBase PH is not liable for any information you choose to include in your recording.

General reminder: Your video will be visible to employers who view your profile. Do not include confidential client information, personal financial details, or contact information beyond what is on your profile. You can update or remove your video at any time.
