2. Employer Access & Pricing Options

WorkBase PH offers two distinct hiring paths. Employers select the option that best suits their needs:

Option 0 — Pay Per Post: $15 per job post

A single job post credit. Goes live immediately upon payment. Employers receive applications directly and manage them through their dashboard. Ideal for one-off or project-based hiring. No subscription required. Job post fees are non-refundable.

Option 1 — The Marketplace: $59/month (Cancel Anytime)

Subscriptions may be cancelled at any time; cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing cycle. Monthly fees are non-refundable.

Option 2 — Elite Headhunting: 20% Placement Fee (One-Time on Successful Hire)

A fully managed, done-for-you search service. WorkBase PH handles sourcing, English proficiency testing, portfolio audits, skills assessments, and preliminary interviews. Employers receive a shortlist of the top candidates only. The process is initiated with a $500 refundable deposit (fully deducted from the final placement fee). The success fee — equal to 20% of the hire's first-year salary — is charged only upon the candidate signing an offer. A 60-Day Replacement Guarantee is included: if the hire does not meet expectations within 60 days, WorkBase PH will conduct a full replacement search at no additional cost.
