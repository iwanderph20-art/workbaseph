1. Scope of Service

WorkBase PH is a curated matchmaking platform — not a job board — that connects Employers with vetted Filipino Specialists. WorkBase PH earns revenue exclusively from Employer subscription plans. Specialists pay nothing and are never charged a commission or platform fee. Every peso earned by a Specialist through a WorkBase PH match belongs entirely to them.

2. Employer Subscription Plans

Employers must maintain an active subscription to access and hire Specialists through the Platform. The following plans are available:

Starter Plan — $49/month

Post unlimited jobs, access to verified talent, basic candidate matching, standard support, and 10–15 active job slots. Optional job post boost available for $9.99. Best for small teams getting started.

Growth Plan — $89/month

Everything in Starter, plus AI-powered candidate matching, priority job listings, direct candidate invites, shortlist management tools, up to 50 active job slots, and the ability to boost up to 10 active jobs for free. Best for growing teams.

Scale Plan — $179/month

Everything in Growth, plus unlimited candidate invites, advanced filters and search, employer branding page, hiring insights and analytics, priority support, a dedicated Talent Success Manager, and a replacement guarantee for hires. Unlimited active job slots with free boost. Best for teams hiring consistently at scale.

Non-Refundable: Subscription fees are non-refundable once the billing period has commenced. Employers may cancel at any time; cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing cycle.

3. Transaction Fees

WorkBase PH charges a platform fee on payments processed through the Platform. Fees decrease as the working relationship continues, rewarding long-term collaboration:

Stage Platform Fee
First payment10%

How it works:

  • The first payment is secured on-platform to ensure a smooth start.
  • As you continue working together, fees automatically decrease.
  • Long-term collaborations unlock one of the lowest rates in the market.

Most platforms charge high fees indefinitely. We don't. Strong partnerships should become more efficient over time.

4. Platform Fee — Legal Disclaimer

WorkBase PH is not an escrow service and does not hold, manage, or receive client funds intended for Specialists. All project payments are processed through trusted third-party payment platforms, ensuring that funds are secured and released directly to the Specialist upon Employer approval for the first completed milestone.

WorkBase PH's role is limited to:

  • Collecting the platform fee from the transaction;
  • Reviewing payments to ensure fair pricing and that the Specialist receives their guaranteed pay;
  • Supporting dispute resolution in cases of breach of contract or other issues.

The full payment for the Specialist is never held by WorkBase PH — it is processed directly through the third-party platform.

Subsequent payments are optional but recommended through WorkBase PH to maintain security, verification, and platform-managed benefits. Employers who re-enter the platform to process additional payments will be charged a service fee of $29 per transaction.

By using WorkBase PH, users acknowledge that WorkBase PH does not assume liability for payments processed outside the Platform. Continued use of the Platform constitutes acceptance of all current fees, terms, and conditions.

The TSM operates within WorkBase PH's internal guidelines and does not constitute a staffing or employment agency relationship. TSM availability is subject to reasonable business hours. WorkBase PH reserves the right to reassign a TSM at any time without prior notice.

6. Replacement Guarantee (Scale Plan)

Scale Plan subscribers are entitled to a one-time replacement of a matched Specialist within 7 calendar days of the engagement start date, at no additional cost, if the fit is determined to be unsuitable. This applies to the first match of each new role and is subject to Specialist availability. It does not apply to terminations caused by changes in project scope, budget, or Employer decisions unrelated to fit.
