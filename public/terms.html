2. Employer Access & Pricing Plans

WorkBase PH offers three self-service hiring plans. All plan fees are charged in USD. Employers select the option that best suits their needs:

Starter — $15 per job post

A single job post credit. Goes live immediately upon payment. Includes 1 active job post, unlimited applicants, free messaging, access to candidate profiles and resumes, standard listing visibility, and built-in interview scheduling. An AI Candidate Audit add-on is available for $15 per post. Job post fees are non-refundable.

Growth — $59/month (Cancel Anytime)

Up to 10 active job posts, unlimited applicants, unlimited messaging, basic search and candidate filters, applicant tracking (pipeline view), standard listing visibility, and built-in interview scheduling. An AI Candidate Audit add-on is available for $15 per job post. Annual plan available at $590/year (2 months free). Subscriptions may be cancelled at any time; cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing cycle. Monthly fees are non-refundable.

Pro — $129/month

Unlimited active job posts, unlimited applicants, unlimited messaging, priority job visibility, built-in interview scheduling, and Unlimited AI Profile Audits (no per-audit fee). Includes all Growth features. Annual plan available at $1,290/year (2 months free). Subscriptions may be cancelled at any time; cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing cycle. Monthly fees are non-refundable.

2a. AI Candidate Audit Add-On

The AI Candidate Audit is a one-time, per-job-post add-on available to Starter and Growth plan subscribers at $15 per audit. Upon payment, WorkBase PH's AI system analyzes applicant profiles against the relevant Job Description and categorizes each applicant as a match or mismatch, providing a brief reason for each mismatch. Mismatched applicants are archived (not deleted) and remain viewable by the Employer at any time. Pro plan subscribers receive unlimited AI Candidate Audits at no additional cost. WorkBase PH provides tools and insights to help employers evaluate candidates — all hiring decisions remain the sole responsibility of the Employer. AI Audit fees are non-refundable once the audit has been processed.
