Payment & Service Fee Agreement — Effective April 1, 2026

By registering or transacting on WorkBasePH ("the Platform"), both Employers and Freelancers agree to the following Payment & Service Fee Agreement. Please read this document carefully before using the Platform.

1. Scope of Service

WorkBasePH provides a digital marketplace that facilitates the connection between Employers and Freelancers. To ensure secure, international fund transfers and milestone protection, WorkBasePH utilizes integrated payment processing through Wise (formerly TransferWise). All financial transactions related to work arranged through the Platform must be processed through this system.

2. Employer Access Fees

The Access Fee: Employers agree to pay a non-refundable Access Fee to post job listings or initiate contact with Freelancers within the WorkBasePH database.

Payment Timing: This fee is charged upfront and is required to unlock the communication portal for specific candidates. It is separate from, and does not offset, any project payments or Platform Service Fees.

3. The Managed Payment Workflow

WorkBasePH operates on a Verified Start protocol. All financial transactions — including initial deposits, milestone payments, and recurring monthly payments — must be conducted through the Platform's secure Wise-integrated system without exception.

Security Retainer (Initial Deposit): Upon hiring and prior to the commencement of work, the Employer shall deposit a Security Retainer equivalent to the first month of the contract (for ongoing roles) or the first major milestone payment (for fixed-price projects) into the WorkBasePH secure holding account.

Ongoing Payments: For subsequent months or milestones, payment must be submitted through the Platform on the agreed-upon schedule. While the Employer is not required to deposit future funds in advance, all payments must still be routed through the Platform's payment system. Direct payments outside the Platform are prohibited.

Disbursement: The Security Retainer and all subsequent payments are released to the Freelancer only upon the Employer's digital "Milestone Approval," "Weekly Timesheet Confirmation," or "Monthly Completion" notification.

WorkBasePH Safeguard: In the event of a sudden contract termination by the Employer without cause, the Security Retainer shall be used to compensate the Freelancer for work already performed, as determined through the Platform's dispute resolution process.

4. Platform Service Fees and Deductions

WorkBasePH maintains the Platform's infrastructure, security, and payment rails by retaining a percentage of the total project value.

Platform Service Fee: A service fee of 5% to 10% will be deducted from the total project amount at the time of disbursement to the Freelancer. The applicable rate is determined by contract size: longer-term or higher-value contracts qualify for the lower rate, while short-term or lower-value contracts are subject to the higher rate.

Net Payment: The Freelancer acknowledges that the amount received in their local bank account or e-wallet will be the total agreed project price minus the Platform Service Fee and any applicable Wise currency conversion costs.

Transparency: The exact fee percentage applicable to each contract will be clearly disclosed within the Freelancer's dashboard before the commencement of work. Freelancers must acknowledge this fee before accepting any engagement.

5. Wise Integration & Currency Conversion

All payouts are processed via Wise. Currency conversion is applied at the rate available at the moment of disbursement. While WorkBasePH uses Wise's published exchange rates, the final amount received by the Freelancer may fluctuate based on real-time currency market values and Wise's own conversion fees at the time of the transfer.

WorkBasePH is not responsible for additional fees that may be charged by the Freelancer's specific receiving bank or local financial institution, nor for exchange rate movements between contract agreement and disbursement.

6. Independent Contractor Status

The deduction of a Platform Service Fee does not constitute an employer-employee relationship between WorkBasePH and the Freelancer. Furthermore, the engagement arranged through the Platform does not constitute an employer-employee relationship between the Employer and the Freelancer. All Freelancers are independent contractors and are solely responsible for reporting and paying their own taxes (e.g., BIR filings in the Philippines) based on the gross income received before Platform deductions.

7. Circumvention Policy

Both Employers and Freelancers agree not to bypass the WorkBasePH payment system to avoid the Platform Service Fee or any other platform obligations. Any attempt to pay or receive funds outside of the Platform for a connection originally made on WorkBasePH — whether through direct bank transfer, third-party apps, or any other means — constitutes a breach of these Terms and may result in immediate permanent account termination for both parties, without refund of any Access Fees paid.

8. Dispute Resolution

In the event of a dispute between an Employer and Freelancer regarding payment, deliverables, or contract terms, both parties agree to first submit the dispute to WorkBasePH's internal resolution process. WorkBasePH's determination in such disputes shall be final with respect to fund disbursement held on the Platform.

9. Amendments

WorkBasePH reserves the right to amend these Terms at any time. Users will be notified of material changes via email or a prominent notice on the Platform. Continued use of the Platform after such notification constitutes acceptance of the revised Terms.
