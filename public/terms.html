Payment & Service Fee Agreement — Effective April 1, 2026

By registering or transacting on WorkBase PH ("the Platform"), both Employers and Specialists agree to the following Terms, Payment & Service Fee Agreement. Please read this document carefully before using the Platform.

1. Scope of Service

WorkBase PH is a matchmaking platform — not a job board — that facilitates curated connections between Employers and vetted Specialists. To ensure secure, international fund transfers and milestone protection, WorkBase PH utilizes integrated payment processing through Wise (formerly TransferWise). All financial transactions related to work arranged through the Platform must be processed through this system.

2. Employer Subscription Plans & Access Fees

WorkBase PH operates on a subscription-based model. Employers must maintain an active plan to access and hire Specialists through the Platform. The following plans are available:

Standard Plan — $29 to start, then $19/month

Unlimited browsing of Specialist reels, direct hiring with no middleman, and access to community support and hiring resources. Best for solopreneurs and early-stage founders.

Growth Plan — $89/month

Everything in Standard, plus priority matching, featured employer status, access to the Verified Talent Only pool, QA Portal, and a dedicated Talent Success Manager assigned to your account. Best for growing businesses.

Pro Hiring Plan — $149/month

Everything in Growth, plus white-glove matchmaking, managed QA oversight, faster turnaround, and a 7-day replacement guarantee. Best for established companies requiring plug-and-play talent.

Non-Refundable Access: Subscription fees are non-refundable once the billing period has commenced. Employers may cancel at any time; cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing cycle. Access Fees paid to initiate contact with specific Specialists are non-refundable under any circumstances.

3. The Managed Payment Workflow

WorkBase PH operates on a Verified Start protocol. All financial transactions — including initial deposits, milestone payments, and recurring monthly payments — must be conducted through the Platform's secure Wise-integrated system without exception.

Security Retainer (Initial Deposit): Upon hiring and prior to the commencement of work, the Employer shall deposit a Security Retainer equivalent to the first month of the contract (for ongoing roles) or the first major milestone payment (for fixed-price projects) into the WorkBase PH secure holding account.

Ongoing Payments: For subsequent months or milestones, payment must be submitted through the Platform on the agreed-upon schedule. All payments must be routed through the Platform's payment system. Direct payments outside the Platform are prohibited.

Disbursement: The Security Retainer and all subsequent payments are released to the Specialist only upon the Employer's digital "Milestone Approval," "Weekly Timesheet Confirmation," or "Monthly Completion" notification.

WorkBase PH Safeguard: In the event of a sudden contract termination by the Employer without cause, the Security Retainer shall be used to compensate the Specialist for work already performed, as determined through the Platform's dispute resolution process.

4. Platform Service Fees and Deductions

WorkBase PH maintains the Platform's infrastructure, security, and payment rails by retaining a percentage of the total project value.

Platform Service Fee: A service fee of 5% to 10% will be deducted from the total project amount at the time of disbursement to the Specialist. The applicable rate is determined by contract size: longer-term or higher-value contracts qualify for the lower rate, while short-term or lower-value contracts are subject to the higher rate.

Net Payment: The Specialist acknowledges that the amount received in their local bank account or e-wallet will be the total agreed project price minus the Platform Service Fee and any applicable Wise currency conversion costs.

Transparency: The exact fee percentage applicable to each contract will be clearly disclosed within the Specialist's dashboard before the commencement of work. Specialists must acknowledge this fee before accepting any engagement.

5. Wise Integration & Currency Conversion

All payouts are processed via Wise. Currency conversion is applied at the rate available at the moment of disbursement. While WorkBase PH uses Wise's published exchange rates, the final amount received by the Specialist may fluctuate based on real-time currency market values and Wise's own conversion fees at the time of the transfer.

WorkBase PH is not responsible for additional fees that may be charged by the Specialist's specific receiving bank or local financial institution, nor for exchange rate movements between contract agreement and disbursement.

6. Independent Contractor Status

The deduction of a Platform Service Fee does not constitute an employer-employee relationship between WorkBase PH and the Specialist. Furthermore, the engagement arranged through the Platform does not constitute an employer-employee relationship between the Employer and the Specialist. All Specialists are independent contractors and are solely responsible for reporting and paying their own taxes (e.g., BIR filings in the Philippines) based on the gross income received before Platform deductions.

7. Circumvention Policy

Both Employers and Specialists agree not to bypass the WorkBase PH payment system to avoid the Platform Service Fee or any other platform obligations. Any attempt to pay or receive funds outside of the Platform for a connection originally made on WorkBase PH — whether through direct bank transfer, third-party apps, or any other means — constitutes a breach of these Terms and may result in immediate permanent account termination for both parties, without refund of any subscription or access fees paid.

8. Talent Success Manager (Growth & Pro Plans)

Employers on the Growth and Pro Hiring Plans are assigned a dedicated Talent Success Manager ("TSM"). The TSM serves as the Employer's primary point of contact for candidate matching, onboarding support, and performance oversight. The TSM operates within WorkBase PH's internal guidelines and does not constitute a staffing or employment agency relationship. TSM availability is subject to reasonable business hours and WorkBase PH's capacity. WorkBase PH reserves the right to reassign a TSM at any time without prior notice.

9. Replacement Guarantee (Pro Hiring Plan)

Pro Hiring Plan subscribers are entitled to a one-time replacement of a matched Specialist within 7 calendar days of the engagement start date, at no additional cost, if the personality or professional fit is determined to be unsuitable. This guarantee applies to the first match of each new role posted and is subject to Specialist availability. The replacement guarantee does not apply to terminations caused by changes in project scope, budget, or the Employer's internal decisions unrelated to fit.

10. Dispute Resolution

In the event of a dispute between an Employer and Specialist regarding payment, deliverables, or contract terms, both parties agree to first submit the dispute to WorkBase PH's internal resolution process. WorkBase PH's determination in such disputes shall be final with respect to fund disbursement held on the Platform.
