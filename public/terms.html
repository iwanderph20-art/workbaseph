A single job post credit. Goes live immediately upon payment. Includes 1 active job post, unlimited applicants, free messaging, access to candidate profiles and resumes, standard listing visibility, and built-in interview scheduling. Add-ons available: AI Candidate Audit ($15/post) and Talent Pool Access ($19/month). Job post fees are non-refundable.

Add-ons available: AI Candidate Audit ($15/post) and Talent Pool Access ($19/month). Annual plan available at $290/year. Subscriptions may be cancelled at any time; cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing cycle. Monthly fees are non-refundable.

Growth — $59/month (Cancel Anytime)

Up to 10 active job posts, unlimited applicants, unlimited messaging, basic search and candidate filters, applicant tracking (pipeline view), standard listing visibility, and built-in interview scheduling. Includes a 7-day free trial for first-time subscribers. Add-ons available: AI Candidate Audit ($15/post) and Talent Pool Access ($19/month). Annual plan available at $590/year (2 months free). Subscriptions may be cancelled at any time; cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing cycle. Monthly fees are non-refundable.

Pro — $129/month

Unlimited active job posts, unlimited applicants, unlimited messaging, priority job visibility, built-in interview scheduling, Unlimited AI Profile Audits (no per-audit fee), and Talent Pool Access included — browse and headhunt specialists directly without posting a job. 2a. AI Candidate Audit Add-On

The AI Candidate Audit is a one-time, per-job-post add-on available to Starter and Growth plan subscribers at $15 per audit. Upon payment, WorkBase PH's AI system analyzes applicant profiles against the relevant Job Description and categorizes each applicant as a match or mismatch, providing a brief reason for each mismatch. Mismatched applicants are archived (not deleted) and remain viewable by the Employer at any time. Pro plan subscribers receive unlimited AI Candidate Audits at no additional cost. WorkBase PH provides tools and insights to help employers evaluate candidates — all hiring decisions remain the sole responsibility of the Employer. AI Audit fees are non-refundable once the audit has been processed.

2b. Talent Pool Access Add-On

The Talent Pool Access add-on is a $19/month optional add-on available to Starter, Essential, and Growth plan subscribers. Upon activation, the Employer gains access to WorkBase PH's curated talent pool and may browse specialist profiles and initiate direct messaging without posting a job. Access is granted for 30 days from the date of payment and does not auto-renew; Employers who wish to continue access must repurchase the add-on. Talent Pool Access is included at no extra cost for Pro plan subscribers. Talent Pool Access fees are non-refundable. WorkBase PH reserves the right to adjust the add-on fee with reasonable notice.

5. Employer Verification

To protect our specialist community, all long-term employer accounts are subject to a quick verification before they can post jobs. Project-based or freelance engagements do not require verification and may proceed directly to plan selection.

During verification, employers are required to submit one document that confirms the legitimacy of their business. The document must include the business name and address. Accepted documents include:

  • Utility bill (internet, electricity, or water) addressed to the company
  • Business License or Articles of Incorporation
  • Tax Permit or Insurance Document (e.g., company liability insurance)
  • Lease or Franchise Agreement
  • Industry-specific or government-issued license

WorkBase PH conducts this verification solely to confirm business legitimacy and protect specialists from fraudulent or bad-faith employers. Verification documents are handled securely and are never shared with third parties. Employers who do not complete verification within a reasonable period may have their ability to post jobs restricted until verification is complete.
