Specialists pay nothing and are never charged a commission or platform fee. Every peso earned by a Specialist through a WorkBase PH match belongs entirely to them.

2. Employer Access & Pricing Options

WorkBase PH offers two distinct hiring paths. Employers select the option that best suits their needs:

Option 1 — The Marketplace: $99/month (Cancel Anytime)

Self-service access to WorkBase PH's full database of pre-verified Philippine Specialists. Employers browse directly, interview independently, and hire with no placement fees or middlemen. Every Specialist has passed WorkBase PH's internal identity and basic English proficiency verification. Subscriptions may be cancelled at any time; cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing cycle. Monthly fees are non-refundable.

Option 2 — Elite Headhunting: 20% Placement Fee (One-Time on Successful Hire)

A fully managed, done-for-you search service. WorkBase PH handles sourcing, English proficiency testing, portfolio audits, skills assessments, and preliminary interviews. Employers receive a shortlist of the top candidates only. The process is initiated with a $500 refundable deposit (fully deducted from the final placement fee). The success fee — equal to 20% of the hire's first-year salary — is charged only upon the candidate signing an offer. A 90-Day Replacement Guarantee is included: if the hire does not meet expectations within 90 days, WorkBase PH will conduct a full replacement search at no additional cost.

To initiate the Elite Headhunting service, Employers pay a $500 deposit. 5. 90-Day Replacement Guarantee (Elite Headhunting)

Elite Headhunting clients are entitled to one full replacement search at no additional cost if the placed Specialist leaves or does not meet the Employer's stated requirements within 90 calendar days of the engagement start date.
